Ingredients

. 1 cup white sugar

. 1/2 Cup unsalted butter

2 large eggs

2teaspoons vanila extract

1 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoons baking powder

2 cup milk

Directions

step1

Gather all ingredients

step2

Precheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees c) Grease and flour a 9-inch square cake pan.

Step 3

Cream suger and butter together in a mixing bowl. add eggs, one at a time, beating briefly after each addition. mix in vanilla.

Step4

Combine flour and baking powerder in a separate bowl. add to the wet ingredients and mix well. add milk and stir until smooth.

Step 5

Pour batter into the prepared cake pan.

Step 6

Bake in the precheated oven until the top springs when lightly touched, 30 to 40 minutes

step7

Remove from the oven and cool completely

Step 8

ENJOY!
